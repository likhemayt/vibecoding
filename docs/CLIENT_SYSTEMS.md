# Client Systems & Business Processes

## Client Intake Process

### Stage 1: Lead Generation & Qualification (Pre-Intake)

**Goal:** Identify qualified leads

**Activities:**
- Inbound inquiries (website, referrals, ads)
- Outbound prospecting
- Referral partnerships
- SEO-generated leads

**Qualification Criteria:**
- ✅ Budget alignment ($2K - $25K+)
- ✅ Project scope fit (websites, landing pages, SaaS)
- ✅ Timeline reasonable
- ✅ Decision maker available
- ✅ Industry/niche fit

**Lead Routing:**
- All leads → Notion CRM Database
- Assign to Project Manager
- Schedule initial call within 24 hours

---

### Stage 2: Initial Consultation (30-45 min)

**Goal:** Understand needs and build rapport

**Meeting Agenda:**
1. Introductions (5 min)
2. Their business overview (10 min)
3. Project goals and objectives (10 min)
4. Timeline and budget (5 min)
5. Next steps (5 min)

**Questions to Ask:**
- What does your business do?
- Who is your target audience?
- What are your main goals for this website?
- What problems are you trying to solve?
- Do you have existing branding/guidelines?
- What's your timeline?
- What's your budget range?
- Who are your main competitors?
- What do you like about their sites?
- Any specific features/functionality needed?

**Outcomes:**
- Detailed notes in CRM
- Sent intake form (email/form link)
- Scheduled follow-up

**Timeline:** Within 1 week of initial call

---

### Stage 3: Intake Form & Discovery

**Goal:** Gather detailed project requirements

**Intake Form Fields:**

**Business Information:**
- Company name
- Industry/niche
- Years in business
- Company size
- Website URL (if exists)
- Location

**Project Goals:**
- Primary goal for website
- Secondary goals
- Success metrics
- Target audience description
- Key value proposition

**Content & Features:**
- Pages needed
- Key features required
- Content availability (ready/need help)
- CMS preference
- E-commerce needs
- Form requirements

**Design & Branding:**
- Brand colors
- Logo (attached)
- Brand guidelines document
- Competitor reference links
- Design style preferences
- Accessibility requirements

**Technical:**
- Domain hosting (have/need)
- Email hosting needed
- SEO important (yes/no)
- Analytics tools
- Third-party integrations
- Performance requirements

**Timeline & Budget:**
- Desired launch date
- Budget range
- Flexible budget (yes/no)
- Payment terms preference

**Decision & Process:**
- Decision maker name
- Approval people involved
- Any constraints
- Previous vendor experience

**Follow-up Activities:**
- Thank you email
- Create project summary document
- Schedule proposal call (3-5 days)

---

### Stage 4: Proposal & Contract

**Proposal Components:**

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Project overview
   - Goals and objectives
   - Key deliverables

2. **Scope of Work**
   - Services included
   - Detailed feature list
   - Timeline breakdown
   - Milestones

3. **Deliverables**
   - Final website
   - Design files
   - Documentation
   - Training/handoff
   - Support period

4. **Timeline**
   - Kickoff date
   - Key milestones
   - Delivery date
   - Post-launch support period

5. **Pricing**
   - Total project cost
   - Payment schedule
   - What's included
   - Optional add-ons
   - Revision policy

6. **Terms & Conditions**
   - Contract length
   - Payment terms (Net 30, 50% upfront, etc.)
   - Revision limits
   - Warranty period
   - Support options

7. **Next Steps**
   - How to accept
   - Contract signing
   - Kickoff preparation

**Contract Template:**
- Master Service Agreement (MSA)
- Project-specific Terms
- Service Level Agreement (SLA)
- IP ownership terms
- Liability and warranties

**Pricing Models:**

1. **Fixed Project Price** (Recommended)
   - Price: $X for complete project
   - Includes: All phases through launch
   - Revisions: 2 rounds of revisions included
   - Timeline: X weeks
   
2. **Time & Materials**
   - $X per hour
   - Estimated: X hours
   - For open-ended/evolving projects

3. **Retainer**
   - Monthly: $X
   - Includes: X hours of work
   - Best for ongoing support/updates

**Proposal Approval:**
- Sent via email with link to sign
- Client signature required
- 50% deposit due before kickoff
- Signed contract filed

**Timeline:** 2-3 days after intake form

## Communication Standards

### Initial Response Time
- Leads inquiries: Within 4 business hours
- Client emails: Within 24 hours
- Client calls: Return within 24 hours

### Weekly Communication
- Status update: Every Monday
- Progress report: Every Friday
- Client check-in call: Weekly (scheduled)

### Project Documentation
- Project brief: Day 1 of kickoff
- Weekly status report: Every Friday
- Design mockups: When complete (Figma link)
- Development milestones: Weekly updates
- Final deliverables: 2 weeks before launch

- Notion workspace for each client

## Revision Policy

### Standard Revisions (Included)

**Design Phase:**
- 2 rounds of revisions per deliverable (wireframes, mockups)
- Major layout changes: 1st round free, then $250/request
- Color/style tweaks: Unlimited in 1st round

**Development Phase:**
- 1 round of revisions per feature
- Bug fixes: Always included
- Feature requests: Separate quote

**Post-Launch (30-day warranty):**
- Critical bug fixes: Free
- Minor tweaks: $50-100 each
- New features: Quoted separately

### Out of Scope (Extra Charges)
- Scope creep (beyond agreed deliverables)
- Additional pages not in proposal
- New features/integrations
- Major design direction changes
- Content creation (unless included)
- SEO optimization (unless included)

## Client Satisfaction & Feedback

### NPS Survey (Post-Project)
- Sent 1 week after launch
- Simple scale 1-10: "How likely to recommend?"
- Open-ended feedback question
- Testimonial request

### Target NPS
- **Goal:** NPS > 50 (8+ rating)
- **Excellent:** 9-10 rating
- **Good:** 7-8 rating
- **Needs improvement:** <7 rating

### Follow-up Actions
- **9-10:** Request testimonial, referral
- **7-8:** Thank you, ask for feedback
- **<7:** Call to understand issues, offer fixes

### Case Study Creation
- Permission from client (required)
- Highlight key metrics and results
- Include before/after screenshots
- Client testimonial
- Share on portfolio
