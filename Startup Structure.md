# AI-Powered Digital Web Agency (Lean Startup Structure)

## Overview

I want to build a small but highly efficient AI-powered digital agency focused on creating modern websites using a team of AI agents and automation tools instead of a large human workforce.

The goal is to create a lean, scalable agency where AI handles most of the work including:

- Planning
- UI/UX Design
- Content Generation
- Coding
- QA Testing
- SEO
- Project Management
- Client Communication

A small human team will supervise, approve, and refine outputs while AI handles the majority of production and operational workflows.

---

# Core Objectives

- Fast website delivery
- High-quality UI/UX
- AI-assisted development workflows
- Scalable frontend architecture
- Minimal repetitive manual work
- Automated operations
- Clean and maintainable codebases
- Efficient collaboration between AI systems and humans
